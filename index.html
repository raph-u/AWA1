Presentation

What 2 Watch is an application written in Java that shows you the information (title, synopsis, actors, etc.) of movies that are stored in your computer.

How to use it?

What 2 Watch has been designed to make it easy for everyone to manage downloaded movies. Here's how it works.

Set a folder

Press the settings button to chose a folder where your movies are located.

Reload

Press the refresh button to scan the folders containing movies.

Select

Click on a movie from the list to display its information.

Features

What 2 Watch is packed with features that will make your life easier!

Rich API

What 2 Watch uses TMDB. This API gathers data of more than 300'000 movies so you don't have to worry about your them being supported.

Intuitive search

Looking for a specific movie but can't remember the name? Don't worry, What 2 Watch search engine got you covered. You can look for movies by release date, actors and even genres!

TV shows exclusion

There's no need to reorganize your video folder! What 2 Watch automatically excludes any TV show that sits in your video folder!

Sleek UI

What 2 Watch UI has been designed to make it easy to use for everyone so you can enjoy your movies straight away.
